# Governance

## Roles

- **Maintainers**: Review/merge PRs, manage releases, and triage issues.
- **Contributors**: Submit issues, PRs, and documentation improvements.

## Decision making

- Day-to-day decisions are made by maintainers in PR review.
- Significant changes should be discussed in an issue before implementation.

## Becoming a maintainer

Active contributors who demonstrate consistency, quality, and good community
citizenship may be invited to join as maintainers.
